Crispy roasted duck with rice

Description

Crispy duck is a delicate roasted speciality. With fine preparation served with delicious rice, sweet chili-sauce and vegetables this meal melts in your mouth.

Ingredients for 1 portion

  • The breast of a duck
  • 250g rice
  • Chili
  • Tomato sauce
  • 1 onion
  • Broccoli

Preparation

  1. Warm your oven up on 160Â°C with curcilating air
  2. Cut the breast of the duck in evenly wide pieces
  3. Shove the duck into the oven and let it bake for 30 minutes until it looks crispy brown
  4. Wash your rice thouroughly several times
  5. Boil 2,5l of water, add salt and your rice and reduce the heat on a low level
  6. Let your rice boil for around 20 minutes
  7. Meanwhile prepare your sauce, cut your onions and the chili after that add them into the tomato sauce and stir everything up
  8. Turn off the stove and let your rice soak in all the water for ten more minutes
  9. Start steaming your broccoli for 10 minutes (this way the broccoli will be more nutritious)
  10. Before everything is finished start warming up your sauce in your pan
  11. Serve your duck with broccoli and your sauce with a fine vine of your choice
