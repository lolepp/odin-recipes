Recipe: Cheese Cake

Description

A cheese cake is a mixture of milk, eggs, flour, sugar and cheese. It is a delicatesse in many households, as just in mine.

Ingredients for 1 portion

  • 250ml milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 30g sugar=1 tablespoon
  • 2 cheese, be careful
  • 400g flour

Steps

  1. Mix milk, sugar and the flour very thouroughly
  2. Add the eggs, mix a little more
  3. Add the cheese
  4. Warm your oven up on 160Â°C
  5. Get a baking pan and fill it up with the mixture
  6. Shove your mixture into the oven, let it bake for 30 mins until it is golden-brown
  7. Enjoy your "cake"
